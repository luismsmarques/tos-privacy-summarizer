The story

Product journey: from idea to launch

A personal journey of turning a simple tip into a complete solution that democratizes access to transparency.

1

Origin story & problem discovery

A post by Pedro Fonseca suggesting using AI to summarize Terms of Service sparked the insight: “What if I could bring this functionality directly to the browser, accessible with just one click?”

“Transform long Terms and Policies into structured summaries and risk ratings, instantly, using AI.”
2

MVP: building fast with AI assistance

With Cursor AI as co-pilot, I quickly built a functional MVP. But I realized that requiring personal API keys was an adoption barrier. The goal was to make ToS Summarizer accessible to everyone and, above all, secure.

How we calculate these scores

Risk level (1–10)
Evaluates data collection scope, sharing practices, security measures and user control options.
Complexity (1–10)
Measures readability, legal-jargon usage, document length and clarity of language.
Best practices (1–10)
Assesses compliance with privacy frameworks like GDPR, CCPA and industry standards.
AI-powered analysis
